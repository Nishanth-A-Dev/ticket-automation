Hi Team,

Regarding your ticket in Gallabox, we would like to inform you that if you are using Razorpay within a bot, the payment details will not be visible in Gallabox.

However, if you are using WhatsApp Native Payments, you will be able to view the commerce payment details directly in Gallabox.

To assist you better, we suggest that you share your use case in a new ticket—specifically how you plan to receive payments from customers and what products or services you intend to offer. Once we have these details, we can guide you more effectively.

Additionally, please note that from the conversation page, you can send payment requests to customers, and the details can be tracked under commerce payments in Gallabox.

For your reference, please find the documentation below:

Request Payment Documentation:
https://docs.gallabox.com/conversations/compose-box

Commerce Payment Tracking Documentation:
https://docs.gallabox.com/payments/tracking-payments
