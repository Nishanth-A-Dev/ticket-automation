Hello Team,

We would like to provide an update regarding marketing template delivery behavior and the error 131049.

Starting March 3, 2025, Meta has enhanced its delivery controls by considering both:

The overall volume of personal and business messages in a user’s inbox
The user’s recent engagement with marketing messages
Based on this, users with low engagement or lower inbox activity may receive fewer marketing messages to maintain a healthy messaging experience. Additionally, Meta has aligned per-user marketing limits with pricing changes, meaning all delivered marketing messages now count toward these limits.

Error 131049 indicates a WhatsApp Cloud API marketing-template delivery restriction enforced by Meta. This occurs when Meta temporarily blocks delivery of marketing templates to specific users to maintain a healthy ecosystem and user experience.

This behavior is not caused by:

Invalid templates
Blocked numbers
24-hour session violations
Marketing Message Limits
Meta applies a dynamic per-user limit based on engagement:

High engagement → Higher likelihood of message delivery
Low engagement → Higher chance of restriction
These limits are internally managed by Meta and are not publicly disclosed.

Reason for the Error

Once a user reaches Meta’s internal threshold for marketing messages, further messages may be restricted across all businesses - even if your messaging frequency is low.

Recommended Actions in Gallabox

To minimize delivery failures, you can use the following features:

Smart Retry (Recommended):

Enable Smart Retry in your broadcast settings
Set a “Retry until” date
Gallabox will automatically retry failed messages up to 3 times
Documentation: https://docs.gallabox.com/broadcast/review-and-send-broadcast#broadcast-reply-settings

Manual Retry:

Configure up to 3 retry attempts manually
Define specific retry intervals
Retargeting:

Use the “Retarget” option in broadcast reports
Select failed numbers and resend after 12–24 hours
Documentation: https://docs.gallabox.com/broadcast/broadcast-re-targeting

Meta Reference:

For more details, please refer to Meta’s official documentation:
https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/templates/marketing-templates/per-user-limits
