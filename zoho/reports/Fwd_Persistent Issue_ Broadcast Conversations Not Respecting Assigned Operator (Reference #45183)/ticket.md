Hi Team,

The template "rev_ultimossemanacliente_19092025" you used for broadcasting contains a Static URL button. When a customer replies to your broadcast, the system considers it as a normal message. As per your default channel settings, Chatbot Vendas was triggered.
For your information, the template should ideally have a Quick Reply button along with the Static URL button. When a customer clicks on the quick reply button, the system will recognize it as a response to the broadcast. Based on your Template Configuration/Broadcast Assignment settings, the conversation will then be assigned to the designated user or team.
We hope this clarifies your doubts. Kindly try a test broadcast using a quick reply button, send it to your number, and then click the template button. You will see that the conversation is correctly assigned to the agent.
