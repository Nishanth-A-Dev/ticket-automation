Hey,

Whenever we use the bot flow card: "Ask Form" the customer/user can still fill out the form after the bot dropout time has been reached. Despite sending them the timeout message the user does not/ will not care about it and proceeds with submitting the form.
This is the primary issue. Secondly, when the user submits the form the message sent after that reads "SENT"
This makes the user believe that their form is submitted successfully, whereas the bot has not registered the inputs of the form since the flow has timed out and the timeout action has occurred (in our case assigned to an agent). 

Either disable the form button after timeout or don't send the "Sent" message giving them the false hope of the form submission.
