## Subject
Feature Request – Assign Conversations to Contact Owner in Broadcast Settings

## Description
Hi Team,

Currently, while broadcasting, we allow clients to assign conversations only to Teams, Users, and Bots. However, there is no direct option to assign conversations to the respective Contact Owner.

As a workaround, we are creating a separate Bot flow and pushing the conversations to the Bot. Then, using the Assign Conversation card, we assign those conversations to the contact owners.

Instead of relying on this additional step, we request you to consider implementing an option to assign conversations directly to the Contact Owner in the broadcast settings. This would streamline the workflow and remove the need for unnecessary workarounds.

I have attached the necessary screenshots for your reference.

Thanks & Regards,
Bharath S
Gallabox Partner Support Team
