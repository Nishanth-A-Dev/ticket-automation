📞 Overview of Calling API Pricing

User-Initiated Calls – No Charge

When a user calls the business via WhatsApp and the business answers using the API, there is no cost involved.

Business-Initiated Calls – Charged to the Business

Pricing is based on:

Call Duration: Measured in 6-second increments (rounded up to the nearest increment)

User’s Country Code: Rates vary by destination country

Volume Tier: Rates depend on total monthly minutes called

Example:

A 56-second call (9.33 pulses) will be counted as 10 pulses.

If a call crosses pricing tiers (e.g., from the 0–50,000 tier to the 50,001–250,000 tier), the entire call is priced at the lower rate of the higher tier.

Call Permission Request Messages

These are considered standard template messages and charged under their respective category (e.g., Marketing or Utility).

Payment Requirement

A valid payment method must be linked to place business-initiated calls.
